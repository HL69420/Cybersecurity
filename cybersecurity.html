What are hacks?

  • Hacks are act of gaining unauthorized access to a computer system or network, often with malicious intent.

    • Types Of Hacks Can Include but not limited to:

    Malware

  • Malwares are a range of hacks used to steal data or spy on people
  • It can range from ransomwares, worms and many more

    • Randsomware Hack

  • A type of hack that restricts access to computer systems or files and demands a ransom payment for their release
  • It typically works by encrypting files, making them inaccessible,

    • Trojan Attack

  • A type of malicious software (malware) that disguises itself as legitimate or harmless software to trick users into installing it.
  • Used to steal data and gain control of devices typically.
  • Extremely difficult to delete without a specialist

    • Denial Of Service Attacks

  • A type of attack which floods the main server rendering it offline.
  • Typically using multiple infected computers controlled by hackers to overwhelm the website server.
