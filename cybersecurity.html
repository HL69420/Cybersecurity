What is Cybersecurity?

  • A practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks.
  • It ensures confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data.

What are Cyberattacks?

  • Cyberattack is unauthorized access into another computer to delete, access, or change content.
  • Done by finding gaps within computer firewalls and using hacks.

Most Common Hacks Include:

  • Malware
  • Ransomware
  • Denial Of Service (DDOS Attack)
